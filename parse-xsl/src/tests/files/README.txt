The aim of this plugin is to gather people information from different sources: copainsdavant, facebook, etc...

There will be information related to:
- people
- wanted people (avis de recherche)



== Copains d'avant ==

=== People ===

Example to reach a people information:

1. http://copainsdavant.linternaute.com/glossary/users
2. http://copainsdavant.linternaute.com/glossary/users/a
3. http://copainsdavant.linternaute.com/glossary/users/a-1
4. http://copainsdavant.linternaute.com/glossary/users/a-1-1
5. http://copainsdavant.linternaute.com/p/dominique-a-6984602

The 4 first points are outlinks that should be browsed but not put into the index.
--> No blacklisting on nutch side otherwise data won't be reached.
The 5th point is the final page to index.
